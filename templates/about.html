About Probiotics

Probiotics are live microorganisms that, when consumed in adequate amounts, provide health benefits. Different strains offer different benefits, making it important to choose the right ones for your specific needs.

Our database includes over 30 scientifically-studied probiotic strains from the Lactobacillus, Bifidobacterium, and Saccharomyces families, along with their documented health benefits.
